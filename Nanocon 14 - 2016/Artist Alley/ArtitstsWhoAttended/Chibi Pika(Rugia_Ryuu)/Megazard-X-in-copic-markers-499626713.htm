Robyn-The-Dragon Featured By Owner Mar 20, 2016
Did you happen to use a color blender by chance? I have been practicing with copics and I have been having trouble making the colors blend and flow into one another. Any suggestions?  
Reply
Chibi-Pika Featured By Owner Jun 1, 2016  Student General Artist
I actually don't use the transparent blender very much!  I just had several different markers of each shade.  (Like, at least 4-5 grays and blues for this one.)  I started with the lightest marker and layered the darker colors over it in the shadowed areas, then went back over it with the light marker and scrubbed the boundary to blend them together while they were still wet.
